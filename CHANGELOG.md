## 0.0.1

* Initial release
* Bluetooth SCO (Synchronous Connection Oriented) audio routing management for Android
* Three core methods: isBluetoothScoAvailable(), startBluetoothSco(), stopBluetoothSco()
* Enables recording from Bluetooth headset microphones by switching from A2DP to SCO/HFP mode
* Automatic stabilization delay for reliable SCO connection
